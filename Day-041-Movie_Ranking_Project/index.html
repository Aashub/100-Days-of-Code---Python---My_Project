Best Picks According To Asteroid Destroyer.

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Fight Club

I love Fight Club because it feels like it’s saying out loud all the frustration, emptiness, and rebellion a lot of us secretly feel about modern life.

Interstellar

I love it because it makes you feel small and emotional at the same time mixing science, time, love, and sacrifice in a way that really sticks with you.

Better Days

I like it because it feels painfully real, showing how pressure, bullying, and loneliness can break someone, but also how one human connection can still save you.

My top 3 Anime of all-time.

One Piece

I love it because it’s not just an adventure it’s about freedom, dreams, and chosen family, and somehow it keeps hitting harder the longer it goes on.

Naruto Shippuden

I like it because it shows how pain, loneliness, and hard work shape a person, and how never giving up can slowly change everything.

Attack on Titan

I love it because it starts like a survival story but turns into a brutal question about freedom, war, and whether anyone is really right or wrong.

My top 3 Web Series of all-time.

Game of Thrones

I love it because no one is safe, power always has a price, and every choice feels heavy with consequences.

Dark

I like it because it makes you feel small time, fate, and family are so tangled that you start questioning free will itself.

Money Heist

I love it because it turns a robbery into a mind game, where emotions, chaos, and clever plans keep colliding nonstop.
